About me

This is me!

My name is Eduardo Páez Rubio and I'm a game developer living in Munich.

I was born and raised in the south of Spain where I developed a great interest for computer science and technology. I graduated with honors in Web Applications Development and migrated to Germany at the age of 20 to get a job.

I have a total of 9+ years experience as a full-stack web developer, game developer and tools programmer.

In my free time I speed-solve Rubik's cubes, design stuff for 3D printers and play video games and board games. And I love participating in game jams!
